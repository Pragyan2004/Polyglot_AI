Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty

At Polyglot AI, we believe your logic belongs to you. We do not store your uploaded images or synthesized code persistently unless you explicitly save them to your history. All OCR processing is ephemeral through our secure Llama-3 parsing engine.

2. Sandbox Security

When you execute code in our workspace, it is run in an isolated E2B Sandbox. These sandboxes are destroyed immediately after execution. Your code never interacts with our internal servers or other users' sessions.

3. Third-Party Integration

We use the following secure partners to provide our services:

  • Groq Inc. — for fast AI inference
  • E2B — for secure code execution

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies solely for maintaining your dark mode preferences and session history within your own browser. No tracking or marketing cookies are deployed.
